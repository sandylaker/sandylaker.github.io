Before I came to Germany, I studied Laboratory Medical Science at Southern Medical University, Guangzhou, China. Then, I switched my major to engineering and studied Electrical Engineering (B.Sc.) at TU Darmstadt. After that, I studied Biomedical Computing (M.Sc.) at Technical University of Munich (TUM). Now I am a Ph.D. student in the SLDS at LMU Munich, supervised by Prof. Dr. Bernd Bischl. My research interests are self-supervised learning, explainable AI as well as their applications in medical domain.
